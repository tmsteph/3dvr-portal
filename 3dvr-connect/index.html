Green Room

Workplace-safe movement, restorative rest, and social creativity spaces.

  • Public/low-friction environments
  • Focus blocks and restorative movement
  • Open participation with low expectation

The model extends to Gold and Red contexts only where both participants have actively consented and confirmed intentions before entering.

Green = workplace-safe

Movement, rest, creativity, and social play with no erotic escalation.

Gold = embodiment & education

Guided touch language, flirtation education, and emotional pacing frameworks.

Red = private erotic play

Strict opt-in only, private context, explicit boundaries, and immediate stop-flow controls.
