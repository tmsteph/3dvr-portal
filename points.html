Frequently asked questions

Can I redeem my points right now?

Not yet. We are currently tracking points while we finish the Stripe integration and legal review.

How will the point value be calculated?

We will divide the Stripe revenue pool by the total active points. Your payout equals the number of points you redeem multiplied by that value.

Will there be bonuses?

Yes. Early adopters and community hosts will receive seasonal boosters once the beta opens. We will also run surprise drops tied to special events.
