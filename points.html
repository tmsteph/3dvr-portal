Every point you earn represents a slice of the monthly revenue collected through the 3dvr.tech Stripe account. When redemptions launch, we will publish the pool size, calculate the value per point, and let you cash in or roll points forward for future drops.

Stripe-linked score economy

Score growth is tied to real revenue while keeping the Stripe balance safe from drains. We enforce a reserve floor so redemptions never empty the account, set daily pacing caps, and add cooldowns between withdrawals to stop rapid outflows.

Buying score

Checkout sessions tagged with your 3dvr.tech identity mint points from the payment amount. If you bring new members, referral links mirror a share of their monthly subscription bonus to your balance.

Selling score

Redemptions move through a review queue with reserve checks, multi-approver thresholds, and short cooldowns. Pending requests stay visible in the ledger until funds are approved and paid out through Stripe.

Subscription boosts

Active subscriptions add recurring score drops every billing cycle. Long-term subscribers unlock growth multipliers that make the shared pot larger for everyone, encouraging renewals over one-time withdrawals.

Frequently asked questions

Can I redeem my points right now?

Not yet. We are currently tracking points while we finish the Stripe integration and legal review.

How will the point value be calculated?

We will divide the Stripe revenue pool by the total active points. Your payout equals the number of points you redeem multiplied by that value.

Will there be bonuses?

Yes. Early adopters and community hosts will receive seasonal boosters once the beta opens. We will also run surprise drops tied to special events.
