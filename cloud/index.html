3DVR / CLOUD

Decentralized Cloud

A local-first, federated alternative to Vercel, GitHub, and closed AI APIs — built from open protocols, runnable on your phone, laptop, VPS, or a friend’s machine.

THE PROMISE
Make centralized platforms optional — without losing the convenience.
  • Deploy from Git, instantly, with previews & SSL.
  • Store code, notes, and assets — sync by default.
  • Compute locally first; pool community resources when needed.
  • AI runs on-device or on trusted relays — not a black box.
  • Federate so your stuff survives outages and censorship.
