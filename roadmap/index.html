Portal loop

Keep the work in one system.

1. Collect signal

Start in the worksheet.

Capture reviewers, objections, questions, and decisions while the wording is fresh.

2. Add structure

Create groups and people in CRM.

Groups hold accounts. People sit under groups. Use the quick lead lane when speed matters.

3. Link the pain

Track problems once.

Log each repeated pain once and link it back to the right people and groups.

4. Work the next step

Push strong records into action.

Use follow-up dates, touch logs, and shared contacts to keep promising records moving.
