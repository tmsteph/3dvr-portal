Weekly breakdown

Run each sprint from one Thursday night meeting to the next.

Tonight

Thursday, March 19, 2026 kickoff.

  • Introduce the campaign and show the current Free, $5/mo, and $20/mo direction.
  • Make it explicit that nobody needs to decide tonight.
  • Leave with the questions, objections, and decision points to settle by next Thursday.

Week 1

March 19-25, 2026

Introduce, listen, and prepare the decision.

  • Share the campaign with the group and anyone already close enough to give honest feedback.
  • Write down the exact questions people ask about the plans, pricing, and promise.
  • Prepare the options that need a decision at the next Thursday meeting.

Deliverable: a decision list for Thursday, March 26, 2026, not a forced decision tonight.

Week 2

March 26-April 1, 2026

Decide the ladder and lock the core copy.

  • Use the Thursday meeting to choose the current Free, $5/mo, and $20/mo framing.
  • Finalize the short pitch for discovery, launch, and growth.
  • Cut competing language from the core funnel pages.

Deliverable: one approved ladder and one reusable short script.

Week 3

April 2-8, 2026

Align the funnel.

  • Make the roadmap, portal, and campaign page point to the same offer sequence.
  • Add the obvious next step from a free homepage into onboarding.
  • Remove dead ends that do not lead to discovery, launch, or growth.

Deliverable: live pages that all tell the same story.

Week 4

April 9-15, 2026

Build the onboarding kit.

  • Write the intake prompt or form for new leads.
  • Draft the homepage setup, launch, and follow-up checklist.
  • Create the welcome and upgrade message templates.

Deliverable: one repeatable onboarding packet.

Week 5

April 16-22, 2026

Run the warm outreach sprint.

  • Reach out to 20 warm contacts across AV, artists, teachers, nonprofits, and local businesses.
  • Track which phrases get replies and which objections repeat.
  • Book short calls or demos with the most ready leads.

Deliverable: five strong conversations and an objection list.

Week 6

April 23-29, 2026

Close the first paid starts and capture proof.

  • Route each lead into Free, $5/mo, or $20/mo based on readiness.
  • Close the first two or three paid members with a fast next step.
  • Collect screenshots, quotes, and before-and-after notes from the first visible wins.
  • Fold the best proof back into the sales script and funnel pages.

Deliverable: proof assets plus a cleaner onboarding checklist.

Closeout

Thursday, April 30, 2026 meeting.

  • Confirm the decisions made between March 19 and March 26 are holding up in real conversations.
  • Confirm whether the five paid-member target was reached and what remains blocked.
  • Update the onboarding checklist with everything learned from the first members.
  • Carry forward the best proof, best script language, and repeated objections into May-June.
