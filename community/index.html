3DVR Community System v0.1

A support layer for builders.

Not social media. A calm builder network where people can clarify their vision, find a small circle, run weekly check-ins, and keep momentum without getting pulled back into conformity.

Three layers

  1. Personal SpaceGoals, notes, and projects stay grounded.
  2. Small Circles3-6 people, honest check-ins, direct help.
  3. Public Builder LayerProgress, collaboration, and open invitations.

Operating rule

What makes this different

  • People are supported in building their vision, not dragged back to default settings.
  • Small circles replace random unsupportive environments.
  • Public sharing is progress-first, not dopamine-first.
  • Every check-in should produce one next move, one ask, or one useful connection.
