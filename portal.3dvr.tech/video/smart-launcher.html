Recommended usage

Keep all four links ready. In real life, the fastest fix is usually dropping one tier.

Everyday

Normal

  • Good Wi-Fi or stable desktop internet.
  • Better motion and image clarity.
  • Still capped lower than a default open room.
Recommended

Low bandwidth

  • Best default for travel and mixed devices.
  • Harder caps on incoming and outgoing media.
  • Usually the best 3dvr room baseline.
Emergency

Survival / Audio

  • Use when the room is falling apart.
  • Tiny video or fully audio only.
  • Best chance of staying connected.
