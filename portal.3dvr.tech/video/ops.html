Preflight

Run this before a real meeting, especially on travel or mobile data.

  • Open the host link first and confirm the control token matches the room.
  • Open one guest link and verify names show under the video tile.
  • Keep the crunched fallback ready in another tab before the meeting starts.
  • Use headphones and close background apps on mobile to reduce echo and throttling.

Failure Ladder

Do not improvise under load. Follow the downgrade order.

  • Step 1: host stays on director, guests drop to low.
  • Step 2: open the crunched fallback link.
  • Step 3: use the shared control token to recommend reset.
  • Step 4: if the room is still unstable, switch the worst device to audio-only from the smart launcher.
