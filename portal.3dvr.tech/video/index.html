Tips:
  • When prompted, enter your display name — it will show under your video for everyone.
  • Use headphones to avoid echo. Close other apps on mobile for best performance.
  • Need louder playback on mobile? Tap the menu ⋮ → Audio Output and select Speaker.
  • The default portal link now uses a simpler two-way room join with label, showlabels, 144p, and a smoother buffer=200.
  • Use Smart Join when you want the page to decide the link for this device and prefer Chrome on Android.
  • Use Meshcast Meeting for the working low-FPS preset you just shared.
  • Use Crunched Video for weak data: 96p, 4fps, very low video bitrate, and a small buffer.
  • Use Smart Launcher when you want the portal to suggest safer room tiers from browser connection hints.
  • Use Meeting Ops when you want one place to generate host, guest, test, and fallback packs.
  • Need more clarity? Switch to Standard Quality (240p/15fps/500kbps) — buffer stays at 100ms.
  • Use Director Mode if you want to manage participants or record streams.
Admin note: avoid &cleanoutput or &cleanviewer if you want names visible.
