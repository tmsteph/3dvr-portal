Release v0.0.55

Week of July 20, 2026

This week turned the portal toward guided change and visible worlds: a private Life Upgrade journey, a clearer Next Move Lab, new language and art destinations, and a weather-aware Sky Room that works across desktop and mobile.

  • Guided personal change: Life Upgrade became a playable, resumable journey with account handoff, encrypted completion sync, reachable question gates, and a small Three.js world across PR #1173 through PR #1188.
  • One-question-at-a-time guidance: Next Move Lab now narrows topics, uses plain language, offers AI guidance, and makes the next action easier to follow in PR #1160 through PR #1168.
  • New places to learn and look: Danny’s Dictionary, the Wenzo art gallery, and the Still Becoming audio-visual experience add focused public destinations, with the supporting agent monorepo and production dependency hardening in PR #1159 through PR #1208.
  • Sky Room: the outdoor scene now responds to location, season, weather, wildlife, daylight, sunrise, and sunset, with fullscreen and mobile controls refined through PR #1189 through PR #1212.
