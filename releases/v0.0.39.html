Release v0.0.39

Week of March 30, 2026

Sales training turns into a daily outreach desk while research, profitability, growth, and onboarding all tighten across the portal.

Overview

This is a dense operator-facing release. Sales training stops pretending to be generic documentation and becomes a daily desk for outreach. Research gets queues, interview tracking, and scheduled handoff. Finance gains clearer profitability signals. The portal home and start flow get simpler language and a more app-like shape.

Sales and research become operational

  • Turn sales training into a daily outreach workspace with a today queue, Gun-backed sync, and cleaner layout.
  • Add a buyer journey stage view, revenue close section, CRM handoff, and a dedicated market research desk.
  • Bring in research playbooks, segment scoreboards, ICP guidance, first-15 interview tracking, and scheduling flow.
  • Log real CRM replies and queue touches so the research and outreach system has better follow-through.

Finance, growth, and operator support

  • Add a profitability desk to sales and align the finance hub around the same metrics and billing counts.
  • Introduce the Growth Lab and homepage growth cron for experiment-driven homepage work.
  • Wire approval-first outreach more tightly into Email Operator.

Home and onboarding clarity

  • Make the portal home more app-like, simplify its intro, and describe entry paths in plain language.
  • Turn the start page into a more direct router for free, paid, and returning users.
  • Use the canonical public email consistently and tighten Debian proot guidance for browser automation work.
