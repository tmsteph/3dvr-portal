Release v0.0.29

Week of December 8, 2025

OpenAI Workbench lead: ship AI-built sites faster with connected GitHub/Vercel guides while Stripe subscriber scoring, admin leaderboards, and guided CRM follow-ups keep rewards and revenue teams aligned.

Overview

This sprint opens with an OpenAI Workbench spotlight so builders can generate, commit, and deploy AI-created sites without losing context. Stripe subscribers dedupe into a live leaderboard, the admin console can search accounts and award points immediately, and the portal surfaces score rewards in the app hub. CRM contributors gain a weekly contact challenge plus a guided touch logger that pairs notes with scheduled follow-ups so revenue teams keep momentum.

Portal quick links

  • OpenAI Workbench – build and ship AI-generated sites with linked GitHub and Vercel guides.
  • Portal home – hop across workspaces and the release hub.
  • Release hub – browse every weekly milestone.
  • Admin – grant points, review subscriber leaderboards, and manage rewards.
  • Sales – keep score cards, top performers, and contact follow-ups in view.
  • CRM & contacts – log touches, schedule follow-ups, and track the weekly challenge.
  • Finance – reconcile ledgers, track Stripe subscriber sync, and review forecasts.
  • Tasks – scan updated summaries and cross-links to calendar items.

OpenAI Workbench spotlight

  • Link the OpenAI Workbench directly to GitHub and Vercel setup guides so developers can ship generated sites without losing context.
  • Clarify vault handling for OpenAI keys and deployment secrets, keeping Gun syncing as the underlying source of truth.
  • Prioritize app search rankings by name to make new directories and builder experiences easier to discover.

Rewards, score, & finance

  • Transform Stripe subscribers into score tallies with deduping by email and immediate refreshes so the leaderboard reflects the latest plans.
  • Surface subscriber score cards and rewards in the app hub so contributors can celebrate the latest balances.
  • Enable admin search and manual point grants from the console while keeping Gun-backed identity as the source of truth.
  • Highlight top sales performers with live leaderboard tiles to keep revenue teams motivated.
  • Organize Stripe plans and safeguards to keep score logic discoverable while aligning with finance summaries.

CRM & follow-ups

  • Introduce a weekly contact logging challenge so CRM owners have a simple target to keep outreach consistent.
  • Add a touch logging dialog that pairs note-taking with follow-up scheduling to keep context attached to every interaction.
  • Keep identity and merge guidance close to contact edits to reduce duplicates while writing back through shared Gun nodes.

Reliability & sync

  • Maintain Gun as the authority for score and contact changes while surfacing deduped subscriber states across devices.
  • Keep dark theme readability and responsive spacing for the release hub and finance views on desktop and mobile.
  • Retain navigational breadcrumbs between admin, sales, and CRM spaces so reviewers can bounce between leaderboards and follow-ups without losing their place.
