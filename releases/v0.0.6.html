Week of May 20, 2025

Async auth & billing

Overview

This week modernized our authentication stack and opened the door to monetization. Async/await patterns now power the sign-in callback, while Stripe billing scaffolding landed in the codebase.

Highlights

  • Migrated sign-in flows to an async architecture for predictable state handling.
  • Cleaned up verbose logging to keep the console readable.
  • Introduced a `stripe.js` module plus metadata for subscription experiments.

Behind the Scenes

The billing integration required a fresh data contract so Gun nodes and Stripe events stay in sync. We also standardized the environment configuration to prepare for production secrets.

Looking Ahead

  • Prototype plan management and checkout experiences.
  • Expand async patterns to the rest of the auth lifecycle.
  • Audit metrics to surface billing status within the admin portal.
