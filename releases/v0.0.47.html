Release v0.0.47

Week of May 25, 2026

This weekly release catches the sprint after v0.0.46: the portal became more operational, more visual, and more connected to revenue, market research, WebRTC reliability, and customer follow-through.

  • Revenue and market systems: Market Lab, Revenue Desk, Growth Operator, Market Pulse, and path-to-profitability docs turned demand research into clearer money actions.
  • Realtime media reliability: WebRTC and Gun live-room work added cache busting, lower bandwidth, TURN support, relay diagnostics, HTTPS fallback, and better signaling waits.
  • Portal surface: homepage search, release hub polish, PWA boot behavior, and the interactive Three.js portal logo made the main entry point feel sharper.
  • Operational capture: Memory Capture, People Log, CRM flow, and Community Farming Network work gave more places to turn ideas and relationships into action.
