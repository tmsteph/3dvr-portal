Week of November 10, 2025

Desktop home experience and a new Tribes-inspired game

Overview

We shipped the first iteration of the desktop-style home hub and launched a fast-paced, Tribes-inspired flight experience that showcases the platform's multiplayer ambitions.

Highlights

  • Delivered the desktop home workspace with quick launches for daily tools.
  • Introduced the Tribes Flight prototype for high-mobility combat practice.
  • Linked new experiences from the main portal so teams can find them instantly.

Behind the Scenes

The home workspace brings desktop metaphors into VR while remaining GunDB-first for persistent layouts. The Tribes Flight prototype doubles as a testbed for low-latency controls and synced scoring so we can expand into squad-based sessions.

Known Issues

  • Contacts (contacts/index.html) does not yet support editing existing entries.
  • The CRM (crm/index.html) mirrors the same limitation and requires a future patch.

Looking Ahead

  • Add editable detail panes to contacts and CRM records.
  • Layer multiplayer lobbies onto the Tribes Flight experience.
  • Continue refining the home workspace layout for tablet and headset users.
