Release v0.0.46

Week of May 18, 2026

This weekly release captures the fast prototype sprint around guest identity, pure Gun media tests, mobile games, and the Nomad Clip hardware direction.

  • Guest identity: upgrade flow work, scoped notes, a migration plan, a new-account fast path, and simpler guest entry points made guest data safer.
  • Pure Gun media: WebRTC and Gun labs explored frames, clips, live rooms, device selection, and local chunk streaming.
  • Mobile playability: Stellar Drift, Ski, and Pong were adjusted for smaller screens and less crowded controls.
  • 3DVR direction: the open-source guide, phone-holder concept app, Nomad Clip roadmap, field-test page, and 3dvr home grid polish pulled hardware and software into one story.
