The 3dvr stack.

A full stack for open computing, open work, and open living.

Layer 1

Portal

Your digital home: dashboard, notes, contacts, CRM, AI, apps, learning, and community.

Layer 2

Agent

The action system: local tasks, outreach, automation, shell commands, deployment, and sync.

Layer 3

Apps

Small useful tools that prove the ecosystem: notes, chat, CRM, files, builder, learning, finance, and health.

Layer 4

Browser

A portal-native browser with workspaces, AI sidebar, PWA management, local agent bridge, and sensory-friendly defaults.

Layer 5

TommyOS

A Linux-based operating environment that makes open-source computing easier for everyday people.

Layer 6

Laptop + phone

Repairable devices, RISC-V experiments, Linux-first workflows, Android/Termux integration, and open hardware direction.

Layer 7

Backpack + mobility

A travel workstation: backpack desk, battery, solar, wearable input, e-bike/scooter, and micro-job field kit.

Layer 8

Clothing + shelter

Linen, wool, repairable gear, tents, yurts, off-grid comfort, and human-centered ecological living.

The next 90 days.

The dream becomes real by shipping small, useful things repeatedly.

Do these first

  • Make portal.3dvr.tech feel like a real home screen.
  • Connect 3dvr-agent to the portal CRM reliably.
  • Create a simple client dashboard for current subscribers.
  • Turn the outreach workflow into a daily sales ritual.
  • Package the Pocket Workstation as a clear demo.
  • Document everything as public learning content.

Do not skip

Do not jump straight to a custom laptop, phone, OS, or car before the portal and agent are useful every day.

The software should earn trust first. Then the hardware becomes believable.

“Each layer should help a real person do real work, learn real skills, and become more free.”

Open by default

Use open-source tools, public documentation, repairable hardware, and teachable systems wherever possible.

Human-first design

Dark-mode-first, low-glare, readable, calm, accessible, ergonomic, and respectful of attention.

Earn the future

Subscriptions, clients, apps, and community must fund the bigger dreams layer by layer.
