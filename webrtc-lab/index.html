Native WebRTC POC

Simple video rooms without vdo.ninja.

A small browser-to-browser conferencing experiment. Gun relays the room signals; WebRTC carries the audio and video directly between participants. Joining now starts media before signaling so peers negotiate with real tracks. The default profile now favors weak mobile links with lower video resolution and bitrate, and the lab can use a TURN relay when configured.

Proof of concept This is mesh WebRTC, not a production SFU. It is best for two or three people while we test reliability.
