Every template links to a live workspace so you can duplicate layouts, save them to your Gun nodes, and share with the team.

  1. Pick a template that matches your workflow and open the linked workspace.
  2. Duplicate the page or card, then customize names, timelines, and data fields.
  3. Share the link with teammates and keep iterating together.
