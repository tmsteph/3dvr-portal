Fascia Release

A low-force reset for neck, jaw, tongue, and computer tension. Keep it gentle. Stop if symptoms intensify.

Start with the downshift.

Tongue soft, jaw heavy, eyes soft. Then move one step at a time.

Downshift first

One hand on chest, one on belly. Inhale for 4, exhale for 6-8. Let the jaw hang.

Tongue soft, jaw heavy, eyes soft.
  • Nose breathing if possible.
  • Six slow breaths.
  • No stretching yet.

The computer jaw rule

Feet grounded. Exhale. Tongue up. Teeth apart. Shoulders drop. Chin gently back. Eyes look far away.

15 seconds every 20-30 minutes.

Lower effort before the clench becomes posture.

Safety first

This is not medical care. Use light pressure and stop if the body pushes back.

Seek care urgently if numbness, weakness, balance trouble, severe headache, dizziness, or injury-related symptoms appear.
Avoid hard cracking, aggressive stretching, deep throat pressure, or forcing the jaw.
Stop and get support worse clicking, locking, pain, changed bite, or symptoms that persist.

Reset complete

Keep the next move small. Teeth apart, tongue resting up, shoulders easy.
