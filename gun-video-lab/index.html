Gun Frame Stream

Video as tiny synced Gun frames.

This experiment does not use WebRTC media tracks. It captures small JPEG frames from your camera, writes the newest frame to Gun, and lets other browsers watch that shared frame key.

Deliberately tiny Use low resolution and low FPS. This is a resilience experiment, not normal video conferencing.
